Barocco.com is the Ukraine region's leading furniture and interior store online. Since 2008, we have expanded to over 200 employees, a turnover of almost USD 1 billion and more than 400,000 satisfied customers. In addition to 11 physical stores, we have e-commerce stores in Ukraine, and Poland.

Barocco.com was started because we found that many furniture and furnishing products in Ukraine were unnecessarily expensive. Many companies buy goods where several intermediaries have paid on the road, which leads to high prices that you as a customer may pay in the end. With the help of good contacts and a small number of intermediaries, we want to offer quality furniture at the best prices on the market.
